Where to Begin

The Sanctuary of Truth

This massive monument, built entirely of wood, is a study of Thai architectural styles. Every inch of the monument is intricately decorated with wooden carvings.

Also known as " The Magnificence of Heaven recreated on Earth, this entire structure was built to pay homage to ancient religions and philosophies ."

Location :206/2 Soi Naklua 12, Pattaya-Naklua, Bang Lamung District, Chon Buri, Thailand

Open: Daily from 8 am to 6 pm

Four Regions Floating Market

The Four Regions Floating Market is one of the best things to do in Pattaya.

It is the world’s largest man-made floating market, at approximately 25 acres.

You can enjoy a boat ride, delicious meals, and even watch a Thai boxing match.

Entrance Fee: 200 Baht ($6)

Timings: 09:00 am – 08:00 pm

Address: 451/304 Mu 12, Sukhumvit Road, Tambon Nong Prue, Amphoe Bang Lamung, Chon Buri

Koh Larn "Coral Island"

Want to escape from the tourist-packed city? Take a break on this peaceful white-sand beach with crystal clear water.

Koh Larn is known for its plentiful coral reefs. Enjoy a variety of water sports, banana boat rides, and underwater sea walking.

Since the island is not far away, visitors can easily go there in the morning and stay for a couple of hours and come back to the city.

Location: Koh Laan, Bang Lamung District, Chon Buri 20150, Thailand
