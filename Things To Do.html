Where to Begin

The Sanctuary of Truth

This massive monument , built entirely of wood , is a study in Thai architectural styles. Every inch of the monument is intricately decorated with wooden carvings. Also known as " The Magnificence of Heaven recreated on Earth , this entire structure was built to pay homage to ancient religions and philosophies ."

Location :206/2 Soi Naklua 12, Pattaya-Naklua, Bang Lamung District, Chon Buri, Thailand

Open: Daily from 8 am to 6 pm

Four Regions Floating Market

The Four Regions Floating Market is one of the best things to do in Pattaya. It is the worlds largest man-made floating market , at approximately 25 acres. You can enjoy a boat ride ,delicious meals and even watch a Thai boxing match.

Koh Larn "Coral Island"

Want to escape from the tourist packed city ? Take a break on this peaceful white-sand beach with crystal clear water. Koh Larn is known for its plentiful coral reefs. Enjoy a variety of water sports , banana boat rides and underwater sea walking. Since the island is not far away , visitors can easily go there in the morning and stay for a couple of hours and come back to the city.

Location: Koh Laan, Bang Lamung District, Chon Buri 20150, Thailand
